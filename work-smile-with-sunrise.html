Case study

Smile with Sunrise — Healthy smiles, stronger community

A brand identity for a family-centered dental clinic in Hastings-Sunrise, East Vancouver, built on the idea that oral care should be gentle, accessible, and community-first — from monthly Smile Check walk-in events to caring communication for all ages.

Role
Brand Designer
Type
Brand Identity
Location
East Vancouver, BC
Deliverables
Full brand system

Understanding the community

The brand was grounded in personas spanning the clinic's real audience — caring parents, independent seniors, curious kids, and busy business owners — each with distinct needs around trust, clarity, and comfort. Mind-mapping around smiles, sunrise, dental care, and community shaped the tagline, logo, color, and voice direction.

A warm, trustworthy identity

Warm orange conveys optimism and community; soft sky blue brings trust and cleanliness. The logo combines an orange heart (compassion), a blue tooth (care without intimidation), and radiating sunrise lines — the idea of starting every day with a healthy smile. Poppins keeps the type modern, friendly, and readable for all ages.

Rules that keep it consistent

Complete usage guidelines — logo dos and don'ts, minimum sizes, whitespace, brand architecture for sub-programs like Kids and Seniors — plus letterhead, envelope, business card, favicon set, a promotional kit, and campaign material for the clinic's grand opening.
