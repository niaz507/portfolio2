Case study

CityKick — Never miss a match again

CityKick is a community-focused soccer platform that makes playing simple, reliable, and fun. It connects players with confirmed matches, nearby fields, and a friendly network of teammates — solving common issues like last-minute cancellations and unbalanced teams.

Role
Brand Designer
Type
Brand Identity
Location
Vancouver, BC
Deliverables
Full brand system

Strategy first

The identity started with research, not visuals: a target persona (a 26-year-old Vancouver player frustrated by cancelled pick-up games), a positioning map against competitors like GoodRec and Urban Rec League, and a value map connecting player pain points to product solutions. The Jester brand archetype was chosen to reflect the fun, social, no-pressure spirit players want from weekend soccer.

The visual system

A warm orange primary (#FF9D42) paired with a teal secondary (#2EB7A6) gives the brand approachability and energy, while Bodoni 72 brings clarity and a confident editorial voice. The circular soccer-ball logo expresses a community-centered spirit, supported by strict usage rules for consistency.

The brand in use

The system was applied across stationery and digital touchpoints: a bold diagonal business card, letterhead and envelope, a slide deck template, and a conversion-focused landing page with clear pricing tiers and social proof.
